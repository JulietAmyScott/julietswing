BE A RESPONSIBLE RIDER. Remember, always wear a helmet, eye protection and protective clothing whenever you ride. Never ride after consuming drugs or alcohol, and never use the street as a racetrack. Inspect your motorcycle before riding and read your owner’s manual. Download the Reimbursement Claim Form for information concerning reimbursement for Motorcycle Safety Foundation (MSF) rider training. Obey the law, use common sense, respect the rights of others when you ride, and make sure you have a proper license when riding on public roads. Operating your motorcycle with a modified engine, emissions-control system or noise-control system may be illegal.

ATVs can be hazardous to operate. For your safety, be responsible. Read your owner’s manual. Always wear a helmet, eye protection and protective clothing and be careful on difficult terrain. All ATV riders should take a training course. Ask your dealer or call ASI at 1-800-887-2887. Never ride after consuming drugs or alcohol, on paved surfaces, public roads, with passengers, at excessive speeds. No stunt riding. Utility ATVs are recommended for riders 16 years of age and older. Respect the environment when riding. Whenever you ride off-road, follow the "TREAD LIGHTLY" guidelines, and always keep on established trails in approved riding areas. Operating your vehicle with a modified engine, emission-control system, or noise-control system may be illegal.

Side-by-Side's (SxS) can be hazardous to operate. For your safety, be responsible. Always wear a helmet, eye protection and appropriate clothing. Always wear your seat belt, and keep the side nets and doors closed. Avoid excessive speed, and be careful on difficult terrain. All SxS riders should watch the safety video "SIDE-BY-SIDE: A GUIDE TO SAFE OPERATION" and read the owner's manual before operating the vehicle. Never drive after consuming drugs or alcohol, on public roads, or with more passengers than the SxS is designed to carry. Pioneer is only for drivers 16 years and older. Drivers and passengers must be tall enough for the seat belt to fit properly and to brace themselves with both feet firmly on the floor. Passenger must be able to grasp the hand hold with the seat belt on and both feet on the floor. Respect the environment when riding. Operating your Side-by-Side with a modified engine, emission-control system, or noise-control system may be illegal.

CRF/R models are designed exclusively for off-road operator-only use in organized, closed-course racing events. Always wear a helmet, eye protection and protective clothing whenever you ride. Never ride after consuming drugs or alcohol. Inspect your motorcycle before riding and read your owner's manual. Use common sense, keep your riding area clean, and respect the rights of others when you ride. Always obey all the laws and regulations governing your riding areas. For more information, visit powersports.honda.com.
